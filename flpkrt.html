    Product Description
    Performance

    With 64 GB of ROM, this mobile will offer plenty of space to store all your photos and personal files. You can also expand the storage space up to 512 GB by using a microSD card. To top it off, it has 4GB of RAM to ensure seamless performance.

    6.1 Max Vision Display

    It has a 15.49-cm HD+ screen with 19.5:9 aspect ratio that will offer maximum viewing, thereby giving life to your favorite movies, sports, and games. Its compact body is also comfortable to hold.

    13 MP Dual Camera System

    Featuring a 13 MP autofocus camera and a 2 MP depth camera, this mobile will let you click stunning photos on the go. It also has a built-in LED flash with 1.12um pixels and a large f/2.0 aperture that will let you take beautiful pictures even in low light conditions. What’s more, you can even take pictures with bokeh effect that will make your portraits stand out.

    8 MP Selfie Cam

    It features an 8 MP front camera with 1.12um pixels, large f/2.0 aperture and a built-in screen flash that will help you take beautiful selfies even when the lighting is poor.

    Fingerprint Reader and Face Unlock

    You can keep this phone protected with both fingerprint and face unlock features. It just needs your fingerprint or your simple glance into the camera to unlock the phone.

    Fast Octa-core Processor

    Thanks to its 2.0 GHz octa-core processor, this mobile phone is highly responsive and it runs smoothly.

    Rapid Charger

    The 10-watt rapid charger of this phone ensures long hours of usage, so you don’t run out of battery while traveling or commuting to work.

    Android 9 Pie

    This mobile comes with the Android 9 Pie operating system to ensure a truly intuitive and ad-free-experience.
