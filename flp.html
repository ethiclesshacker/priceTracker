    Description

    If you are a travel blogger, gamer, entertainment seeker, or a person who loves a high-end personal device, then the Redmi 8 has been created to meet your needs. This smartphone features a 15.8-cm (6.22) Dot Notch Display, a 12 MP + 2 MP AI Dual Camera, and a 5000 mAh High-capacity Battery to offer detailed views of the stunning photos that you can click all day long without running out of battery life.

    Product Description
    AI Dual Camera

    The Redmi 8 comes with a 12 MP AI Dual Camera that features a 2 MP Depth Sensor, f/1.8 Aperture, 1.4μm Pixel Size, a Dual PD AI Portrait Mode, and an AI Scene Detection function to let you click life-like photos with impressive details.

    8 MP AI Selfie Camera

    The 8 MP AI Selfie Camera is boosted by an AI Selfie Portrait Mode to click flawless selfies. Also, the AI Face Unlock function lets you unlock the phone with just a glance.

    High-capacity Battery

    You can easily charge the 5000 mAh battery using the Type C Port, and it supports 18 W Fast Charge. So, you can enjoy playing games, listening to music, and watching videos all-day-long without running out of battery life.

    Stunning Display

    The 15.8-cm (6.22) High-definition LCD Display comes with a Dot Notch Design and Corning Gorilla Glass 5 protection. As a result, you can experience immersive visuals on the sturdy display.

    Aura Mirror Design

    This is a stunning smartphone that comes with the Aura Mirror Design for a premium look. Also, it has a fingerprint scanner for easy unlocking. Furthermore, the nano-coating by P2i makes it a splash-proof smartphone.

    Fast and Powerful

    Powered by the Qualcomm Snapdragon 439 Octa-core Processor which comes with up to 2.0 GHz clock speed, the Redmi 8 lets you switch between tasks and apps easily. Also, you can seamlessly enjoy HD content and play HD games on this powerful device. The Redmi 8 also comes with a 2+1 SIM card slot along with a dedicated micro SD slot to expand memory up to 512 GB.

    Enjoy Your Music

    The Redmi 8 features a Wireless FM Radio and a 0.82CC Acoustic Chamber to let your favorite music take over your world. This smartphone also comes with a dedicated IR Blaster to let you control multiple devices with a simple tap on the Redmi 8.
